JAMES HICKS

I'm a junior full stack developer whose journey is paved by an explorative nature...



This key quality enables me to search for answers and solutions, unwavering at the size of each figurative mountain, and drives me up the slopes with a force that grows along with the peaks that are conquered. With the tools available, I create expeditions to lead myself or a team to the peaks with a clear path that ensures we return with the results of our master's request. Currently, from base camp at Coder Academy, I ascend the peaks daily; though as its range is limited I must find a new base camp to continue to explore and ascend to higher peaks...

For years I stood divided...



Previously I worked in warehouse environments, in order to fund my personal expeditions as a musician. My growth was a slow process in these two realms, however, as I lived in a world of black and white; but through exploration I found colour beneath the surface. I realised I could find a career path that feeds my curiosity and creativity, and holds certainty in presenting mountains higher than I'd ever overcome; so that I may grow along with them. I'm in the tech world now, ever exploring...
